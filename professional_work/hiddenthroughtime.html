Hidden Through Time

Hidden object game where you are taken through five big time ages.

This was the project I started on at Rogueside. I joined the team one month before the release and was given the opportunity to learn what it takes to be part of a professional team.

I helped with bug fixing the input system as well as porting to mobile devices and tvOS.

This game became one of the most popular games made by Rogueside. It has an active playerbase and multiple DLCs were released.
