Best Served Cold

Best Served Cold is a gripping visual novel detective game featuring a tale of revenge, intricate characters, and branching narratives.

As the team lead on this project, I had the opportunity to contribute to multiple aspects of the game. I worked on refining the dialogue system to ensure dynamic, engaging interactions and developed a unique drink-mixing mini-game that tied directly into the narrative.

This project allowed me to deepen my understanding of Unity's event-driven systems and improve the integration of narrative tools into gameplay. Additionally, I focused on optimizing our workflow, enhancing team collaboration, and streamlining the implementation of new content.

We aimed for a polished experience across platforms, including PC and consoles, while staying true to the compelling storytelling at the heart of the game.
