Warhammer 40,000: Shootas, Blood & Teef

2D action shooter set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. The player is an Ork who is searching for his hair squig.

This was a 2+ year development process that I worked on from start to finish. From networking to weapons and AI, I contributed to all aspects.

The game is available on all platforms, this gave me the opportunity to expand my porting skills for all platforms.

During the development of the game I created an interest in tool development. For instance, I created a tool that allowed us to create and edit 2D AI navmeshes. Most of the other tools for this project were to speed up the development pipeline.
